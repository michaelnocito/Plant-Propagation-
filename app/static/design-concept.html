Glasshouse — daylight botanical

A bright Scandinavian conservatory: warm bone-white surfaces, one living-green accent, and a terracotta value note. Where v9 went dark and v10 went pressed-herbarium, this leans clean and airy — generous whitespace, an opsz serif for headings and big numbers, and a signature “grow-line” range bar that sketches a sprig instead of a flat track.
